Shenao Zhang

I am a final-year Ph.D. student at Northwestern University advised by Prof. Zhaoran Wang. Previously, I interned at Apple, Google, Microsoft, ByteDance Seed, and Tencent AI.

My research centers on LLM and RL, with a focus on reasoning, agents, and alignment. I develop models that learn strong foundations from offline data and self-improve via online exploration and adaptation. Currently, I'm particularly interested in designing fine-grained and scalable supervision for LLM training.
